2. Transaction Segmentation

RFM-Style Behavioral Feature Segmentation

Framing

Transaction segmentation usually starts with customer-level feature engineering rather than raw order lines. Common features include:

  • recency,
  • purchase frequency,
  • monetary value,
  • average order value,
  • discount reliance,
  • category breadth.

This section generates synthetic RFM-style data plus two additional behavioral variables.

Interpretation

A typical business reading might be:

  • high-recency, low-frequency, high-discount customers are at-risk bargain buyers,
  • medium-value and medium-frequency customers are core mainstream buyers,
  • low-recency, high-frequency, high-spend customers are premium loyalists.
